Her Story

A Life of Grace & Faith

Margaret Darkwaa Owusu was a woman of extraordinary grace, unwavering faith, and a love that knew no bounds. Born in 1966, she devoted 60 years to her family, her community, and her God — leaving behind a legacy that will forever echo in the lives she so tenderly shaped.

A pillar of strength and warmth, she was the heartbeat of her home. Her laughter was infectious, her wisdom deep, and her embrace a sanctuary for all who knew her. Whether in the kitchen, the church pew, or the community hall, Margaret's presence was a blessing.

She raised her children with devotion, served her church with dedication, and offered her friendship freely to all. Her memory is not a wound — it is a treasure, a heritage, a light that does not go out.

She rests now in the arms of the God she so faithfully served. And we, who carry her forward, do so with gratitude, with pride, and with love.

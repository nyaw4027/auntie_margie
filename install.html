Install on Your Phone

Add the Auntie Margie memorial app to your home screen for quick access — no App Store needed.


Android · Chrome / Samsung Internet
1
Open the site in Chrome
Visit the memorial site in Google Chrome on your Android phone. Other browsers like Samsung Internet also work.
2
Look for the Install Banner
After a few seconds, Chrome will automatically show a popup at the bottom of the screen:

Add Auntie Margie to Home Screen
3
Tap "Install" or "Add"
Tap the button and confirm. The app icon will appear on your home screen immediately.
4
If the banner doesn't appear…
Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in Chrome's top-right corner, then tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App".
⋮ Chrome Menu → Add to Home Screen
Once installed, the app works offline. You can view the programme, tributes and condolences even without internet.

iPhone & iPad · Safari
Important: This only works in Safari. If you're using Chrome or another browser on iPhone, switch to Safari first.
1
Open in Safari
Open Safari on your iPhone and navigate to the memorial site URL.
2
Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share button — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up: ⬆
⬆ Bottom toolbar → Share button
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
In the share menu, scroll down until you see "Add to Home Screen" and tap it.
Share menu → Add to Home Screen
4
Tap "Add" to confirm
You'll see a preview with the app name. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner to confirm. The app icon will appear on your home screen.
On iPhone, the app opens in full screen, just like a native app — no Safari address bar.

Tributes

Heartfelt tributes from those whose lives were touched by Margaret Darkwaa Owusu — "Auntie Margie".

My dearest Margie, Forty years ago you walked into my life and made it complete. Every morning with you was a blessing I never took for granted. You were not just my wife — you were my prayer partner, my best friend, my home. Your laughter filled every room. Your faith anchored our family through every storm. You cooked, you prayed, you loved — with everything you had. They say time heals all things, but how does one heal from losing their very heartbeat? I do not grieve as one without hope, for I know the God you served has received you into His arms. And until we meet again, I will carry you — every single day — in my heart. Rest well, my love. You ran your race faithfully. Well done.
Mr. Emmanuel Owusu

The kitchen feels different without the smell of your cooking. But your love — that is something that does not fade. We carry you forward, Mum. Into everything we do. Forever your children.
